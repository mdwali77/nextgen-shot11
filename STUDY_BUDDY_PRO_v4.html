💡 JEE Preparation Guidance
📅 12-Month Roadmap
Month 1-4: Complete NCERT + Basic concepts (Phy, Chem, Maths)
Month 5-8: Advanced topics + DPP daily + PYQ chapter-wise
Month 9-11: Full-length mocks every week + weak chapter revision
Month 12: Revision only — no new topics, mock every 2 days
📌 High-Weightage Topics
⚡ Physics: Mechanics, E&M, OpticsHigh
⚗️ Chem: Organic, ThermodynamicsHigh
📐 Maths: Calculus, Coordinate GeometryHigh
⚡ Physics: Modern Physics, WavesMedium
📐 Maths: Vectors, 3D, ProbabilityMedium
🏆 JEE Advanced Strategy
Paper Pattern: Multi-correct, Integer, Match Matrix — no fixed pattern, read carefully each year
Physics: Focus on concepts — Mechanics, Electrostatics, Modern Physics. Irodov for practice.
Chemistry: Organic mechanism mastery is key. Physical chem numerical fluency essential.
Maths: Strong calculus + Algebra. Attempt only if confident — negative marking is harsh.
📚 Recommended Books
⚡ HC Verma — Concepts of Physics Vol 1 & 2
⚡ Irodov — Problems in General Physics
⚗️ J.D. Lee — Inorganic Chemistry
⚗️ Morrison & Boyd — Organic Chemistry
📐 Cengage / Arihant — Mathematics Series

💡 Study Planning Tips
⏱️
Pomodoro Technique: Study 45 min, break 10 min. Keeps focus sharp throughout the day.
🧠
Spaced Repetition: Revise a topic after 1 day, 3 days, 7 days, then monthly.
📋
DPP First: Always solve DPP before checking notes. Active recall > passive reading.
🎯
Mock Tests: Take 1 full mock every week. Analyse before starting new topics.
😴
Sleep 7-8 hours: Sleep consolidates memory. Don't sacrifice sleep for study.
📱
Use Focus Mode: Block phone during study slots. Use PW videos only for study.
